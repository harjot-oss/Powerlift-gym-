Our Philosophy

We Don't Train Bodies.
We Build Athletes.

PowerFit was built in a 4,000 sq ft warehouse with second-hand barbells and one shared vision: create a training environment where ordinary people achieve extraordinary things.

Today we span three floors, 28,000 square feet of world-class equipment, and a community of over 12,000 members — from first-timers to competitive powerlifters.
